Blockchain - The Technology Of Future

Project Summary

1) Vision : Create awareness of Blockchain Technology, Cryptocurrencies, NFTs, Web 3.0, etc. among the middle-class Gen X people and the upcoming generations for the welfare of our whole civilization.

2) We have seen how investing in Cryptocurrencies can scale up our profits by a huge margin (eg. some people have become crypto billionaires) so, we can pull up the people from backward communities / those having low socio-economic status, increase the earnings of middle-class tax bearers, and thus, can bring huge prosperity.

3) Solution :
• Create a chatbot that will provide all the information related to the concepts of Web 3.0, the chatbot will be embedded into a website.
• The website will be a one-stop destination, the embedded chatbot will act as a service provider where a user can clear all his doubts, from the basic ones such as what is an investment to the advanced concepts of Crypto mining.
• The website/chatbot will be accessible to the remote areas where the internet is available (even with high latency where playing videos is a problem) and people can understand state-of-the-art technology related to finance i.e. Blockchain in an easy and simple language.

• Azure Services Used :
a) QnA Maker : To build a Knowledge Base
b) Azure Bot Service : To create chatbot
c) Static Web Apps : To host website (chatbot embedded)

4) In this project, I am using 3 services provided by Microsoft Azure, VS Code, and GitHub. This project mainly will be a solution for the people who are unaware of the potential of Web3, Crypto, etc.
